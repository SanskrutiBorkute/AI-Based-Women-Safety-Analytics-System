# 📸 Project Gallery

## AI-Based Women Safety Analytics System (SafeHer)

This gallery showcases the major features and user interface of the AI-Based Women Safety Analytics System.

---

## 1. Login Page

The login page provides secure authentication for registered users before accessing the application.

![Login Page](screenshots/login%20page.png)

---

## 2. Registration Page

New users can create an account by providing their name, email, phone number, and password.

![Registration Page](screenshots/register%20page.png)

---

## 3. Dashboard

The main dashboard displays the overall safety status, AI safety score, current location, network status, emergency contacts, and notifications.

![Dashboard](screenshots/Dashboard%20page.png)

---

## 4. Live Location Tracking

Displays the user's live GPS location using Leaflet Maps along with nearby hospitals, police stations, danger zones, and AI-generated safety overlays.

![Live Map](screenshots/Live%20Map%20page.png)

---

## 5. SOS Emergency Page

Users can instantly trigger an emergency SOS alert to notify trusted contacts and initiate emergency assistance.

![SOS Page](screenshots/SOS%20page.png)

---

## 6. SOS Alert Confirmation

Confirmation popup displayed after successfully sending an SOS alert with the user's live location.

![SOS Alert](screenshots/SOS%20alert.png)

---

## 7. AI Risk Analysis & Safe Route Recommendation

Shows AI-generated risk prediction, crowd density, destination analysis, travel time estimation, and the safest recommended route.

![AI Risk Analysis](screenshots/AI%20Risk%20Analysis%20and%20Safe%20Route.png)

---

## 8. AI Trend Analytics & Recommendation

Displays historical AI safety trends, confidence scores, model explanations, and intelligent recommendations generated by the machine learning model.

![AI Trend Analytics](screenshots/AI%20Risk%20Trend%20Analysis%20and%20Recommendation.png)

---

## 9. Trusted Contacts & Nearby Support Network

Allows users to manage trusted contacts and view nearby hospitals and police stations for emergency assistance.

![Trusted Contacts](screenshots/Contacts%20and%20Nearby%20Users.png)

---

## 10. Voice SOS & Auto SOS Monitoring

Supports voice-based emergency activation, automatic SOS monitoring, profile analytics, and session report generation.

![Voice SOS](screenshots/Voice%20SOS%20and%20Auto%20SOS%20monitoring.png)

---

## 11. AI Safety Assistant

Built-in AI chatbot that provides safety guidance, emergency assistance, nearby support information, and answers user safety-related questions.

![AI Assistant](screenshots/AI%20Assistant.png)




**Project Name:** AI-Based Women Safety Analytics System (SafeHer)

**Technology Stack:** Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQLite, Scikit-learn, Leaflet.js, OpenStreetMap
